# Company Overview

## What is Spur?

Spur is an AI-powered customer engagement platform that helps businesses automate customer support, sales, marketing, and lead generation across multiple communication channels.

The platform combines AI Agents, automation workflows, CRM capabilities, and omnichannel messaging into a single solution.

# Core Product

## AI Agents

Spur AI Agents provide conversational automation while performing business actions.

Capabilities include:

* Answer customer questions
* Retrieve information
* Execute business workflows
* Book meetings
* Update customer records
* Integrate with external systems
* Escalate conversations to humans

Unlike basic chatbots, Spur AI Agents can perform actions rather than only responding with text.
